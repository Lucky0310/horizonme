INTRODUCTION

“HORIZONME” is part of a Specialized Consulting Contracting group. IT is an expanded company and continuation to a previous engineering firm, it was established Y2022 to continue providing Services & support to it group and customers in many different business fields. Its focus on delivering engineering & technical consultation, and project management that contributing to localize industries, products & service in KSA.
